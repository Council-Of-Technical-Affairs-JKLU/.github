The **Council of Technical Affairs (CTA)** is the central technical backbone of **JK Lakshmipat University (JKLU)**. We are dedicated to transforming JKLU into a premier technology hub by bridging the gap between theoretical learning and real-world application.

We don't just write code; we power the campus. From deploying full-stack applications to setting up concert-grade audio systems for university festivals, CTA handles it all.

## ‚öôÔ∏è What We Do

We operate on two major fronts: **Innovation** and **Infrastructure**.

### 1. üíª Innovation & Development
We are the incubator for student projects. We turn ideas into deployable software and hardware.
- **Hackathons:** Organizing coding battles to sharpen skills.
- **Open Source:** Encouraging contributions to the global dev community.
- Enables interdisciplinary collaboration
- Organises workshops on emerging technologies

### 2. üîß Infrastructure & Audio Engineering
We are the crew behind the curtains. If an event sounds good and looks good, CTA is on the console.
- **Audio Engineering:** Live mixing, sound balancing, and array management.
- **Stage Logistics:** Setup of microphones, amps, and musical instruments.
- **Inventory Management:** Maintaining the university's high-end technical assets (Mixers, Consoles, Lighting).
